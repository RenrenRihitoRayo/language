"mu" is asking if you are doing a certain action
"wan" is what
"ven" is when (german accent of when)
"pano" is how (tagalog, pano)
"ha" is where
"naze" is why
"mo" is just a marker

mo is used when the sentence isnt a question (can be a question) but said in a tone of a question, for confusion.

"mo mut?" => "no?"
or for asking agreement or responding
"mo ja?" => "yeah?"
